## 🧬 About

`CSE undergrad` · `MERN stack` · `QA & documentation` · `editorial photo-editing on the side`

I'm a Computer Science Engineering student who likes systems that are easy to test as much as systems that are easy to build. Most of my recent flight time has gone into **SMG-JUNE-2026**, a MERN-stack Employee Management Portal — I work across QA, bug-fixing, and documentation, with a strong opinion that nothing gets marked "tested" unless it's actually been tested.

Outside of code, I shoot bold, moody, editorial-style travel photography and edit it together in CapCut/Canva — same instinct for precision, different medium.
